Internal UI for Insurance Hotline

Moscow State Insurance Fund runs a hotline: operators answer insurance-related questions, explain what rights patients and doctors have, or arrange a doctor appointment. I spent a couple of days watching the operators onsite and discussing their job with them, and analyzed calls data. I understood what additions will be useful in the redesigned UI (most popular calls topics, autocomplete for cities and insurance companies) and eventually persuaded the client to get rid of unnecessary mandatory fields (eg, separated First and Last name).

If there's a serious problem, it's usually solved by a an expert. A request is transferred to them, and the most important data there is its topic and a person's name and phone number. The experts are busy with a lot of work unrelated to the hotline requests, so they don't monitor the system, but could set up browser notifications.

A supervisor makes sure that all the incoming calls get answered. The unfulfilled requests are highlighted in the list.
